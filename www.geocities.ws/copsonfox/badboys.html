Lyrics

Lyrics to the theme song by reggae group by Inner Circle. There are three versions. The pilot episode is the only one to have the full song, the lyrics below are the most common 1 minute intro and the 30 second intro version used in reruns is just the chorus. The first season listed the members of Inner Circle in the credits. They released music video for 'Bad Boys' in 1993 that incorporated Cops footage. In 1993 the song won a Grammy Award and in 1999 singer Calton Coffie wrote a sequel called "Ruffa Than U".

Music Video

Opening shot with title

The video is 3:46 from 1993. It opens with a shot of Cops playing on a TV in the street. Shots of the group are shown with each member in black and white in the dark and someone shining a light on them. Three guys walk on the street in color which mixes with shots of the band in color playing a concert. Shots alternate from clips of Cops on the TV to the band in B/W, playing live and the three guys. One of the guys climbs up a rafter as the band walks around. The guy gets down, a girl with long braided brown hair dances around the street, the guys run for it and fight, cops pull up by the guys and one of the guys climbs a fire escape. Clips alternate with the girl, the band, TV clips and the kid. Near the end the singer shines a light into the crowd at the concert. B/W shots of the band walking away.
