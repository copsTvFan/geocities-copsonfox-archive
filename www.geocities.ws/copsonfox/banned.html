Banned Episodes

If you are looking for these episodes on DVD then email me.

I started watching and recording the show since it first started. Then right before Season 16 started I wanted to rerecord all my old episodes to upgrade to a higher quality when they reaired. What I found out were that certain episodes are never shown, 2 have been changed, an hour episode was only reshown as a half hour, one had 2 segments removed and the third recycled on another episode. Later there was even an episode announced that was never shown, but to this day is still listed in the reruns and is always something else. There has never been an official statement about banning an episode, so I went back and watched all of them and the underlying thing I found was all of them had extremely bloody segments and murders.
